20 × 40mm · fits 3ml peptide vials · Niimbot & compatible label printers

How to Use the Label Generator

  1. Enter your source — The source you purchased your peptide from.
  2. Fill in manufacturer, peptide name, size / amount, and date.
  3. Optionally add a dosage amount and units, and a URL to generate a QR code.
  4. Click Generate Label to confirm, then Download JPEG.
  5. Print at 100% scale on a 20 × 40mm label using Niimbot or any compatible label printer. This size fits perfectly on all 3 mL peptide vials.
